Welcome to Nairobi

Widely know as the green city in the sun, Nairobi is the perfect combination of city life and wildlife. Nairobi is the capital city of Kenya and the most cosmopolital city in East Africa. Yet just a few miles from the jungle of building, lies the famous Nairobi National Park. Nairobi is the only city in the world to have a game park within in the city area. Thus making it a very unique country offering two different worlds that are rarely found together.

Quick Facts on Nairobi

Nairobi has a population of around 4.9m people. It is a relatively young city, since 1954, but is rapidly growing. Nairobi is a vital commercial and financial regional hub, home to the regional headquarters of various major international companies and organizations.
The weather is pleasant and tropical since the city lies right on the equator. It mostly warm with low levels of humidity.
The main languages spoken in Nairobi are Swahili, English and a combination of multiple languages known as "sheng". Most locals in the city understand english enough to communicate with visitors.

Cultures

Kenya is home to 42 different tribes. With each of these tribes comes different cultures, different food, languages, and beliefs. Nairobi being the capital city is where all these different tribes combine. Nairobi is the best place to experience a piece of everything that Kenya has to offer.

The main foods to try are ugali, a staple food in kenya. Nyama Choma, goat cooked on a grill and the holy grail of Kenyan food, "mutura".
